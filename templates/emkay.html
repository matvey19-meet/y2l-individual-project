Emkay

For starters, what's up with the name. Emkay is just a spelling of my intials, and then it became a nickname for me (but people started to use matman now for some reason...). It's my signature, so if you see that somewhere, it means I did it. But it also defines my world, which you will learn about on this page. So I am welcoming you to go inside the head of Emkay.

the story

So let me start with a bit of background about me, I am Matvey Khoussainov, I was born in Israel to Russian parents, spent 8 years of my life here. In 2011, my family moved to Montreal, Canada, to look for a better life. In the summer of 2016, I have made the choice to move back to Israel. It's stil unclear why exactly, the reasoning goes from "patriotism" to Israel, to me just trying to escape my life in Canada. And since that moment, everything changed...

From the moment I moved until now, I have changed greatly. I got freedom. And it opened my mind. Everything was a possibilty, there was nothing I wouldn't be willing to try, to do. Every choice I made, although some are not that great, it made me who I am today.

Through out the years, I worked on myself to help me express myself. That's how I got into design, visual arts, music and many more. It was theropeudic, I wasn't in a good place at the start, but now, I have achieved peace of mind. So I have created this space to showcase what I did to achieve this, and maybe help you find motivation to believe in yourself, to never stop doing what you love and that anything is possible..

yeah I know what I said is cliche, but hell, it works...

For my last words, I will say this; Dont't give up. Don't let your demons eat you. Look at bigger picture, and maybe you'll find something new, about you, about someone else. Know that life can change directions, even when you ain't planning it. Because I believe in you, because if I can, you can. There will be always a person that loves, hell, I love you. Now get out there, you already spent to much time here. Thank you for visiting, and you are free to come back anytime you want.
